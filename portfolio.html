Hello, my name is Ronak Guliani

Welcome! I am a software engineer currently studying Computer Science and Physics at Portland State University. I am a creative problem solver with a passion for design and technology. My love for technology began at a young age, where I would spend hours tinkering with computers and software, trying to figure out how they worked. As I grew older, I realized that my passion for technology went hand in hand with my love for design.

Over the years, I've gained experience in all aspects of the software development process, from ideation and prototyping to testing and deployment. I have a keen eye for detail and strive to create software that is not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing and easy to use.

On my page, you'll find a selection of projects that showcase my skills and experience in software. From web development to user interface design to data analysis, I'm always pushing myself to create work that is both innovative and user-friendly. I'm excited to share my portfolio with you and look forward to connecting with others who share my passion for technology and design!
